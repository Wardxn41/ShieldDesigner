Projection Export
This page maps your shield design into a projector-ready image:
• Curvature compensation (cylindrical scutum)
• Keystone alignment (4-corner handles)
• Scutum outline overlay for edge alignment
Controls
• Drag a corner handle to keystone-fit your projector
• Drag inside the quad to move the whole design
• Mouse wheel inside quad to scale
• Hold Shift while dragging inside to rotate (optional)
